Fanchone

Ingredients

  • Dried rice noodles
  • Vegetable oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Diced Onion
  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken breast meat
  • Thinly sliced cabbage
  • 4 thinly sliced carrot
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 lemons - cut into wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Place rice noodles in a large bowl; cover with warm water and let soften for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in chicken, cabbage, and soy sauce. Cook until cabbage begins to soften.
  4. Toss in noodles and cook, strirring constantly, until heated through
  5. Transfer pancit to a serving dish and garnish with lemon wedges
